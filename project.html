Project Details

Project Name:Mobile phone signal jammer for GSM, CDMA & 3G Network using prescheduled timer.
Team size: 4
Project Description:

Mobile Cell Phone Jammer also known as Mobile Phone Jammers is a device that jams or blocks the communications between the cell phone and the communication tower of the service provider thus making your mobile phone as idle with "No Service" indicator,and all incoming calls are blocked as like if your mobile phone is switched off.
